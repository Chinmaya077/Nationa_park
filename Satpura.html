SATPURA NATIONAL PARK

  • It is inside the premises of the district of Hoshangabad well dispersed over an incredibly large plot that measures approximately
    524.27 square kilometers. .

  • The Satpura National Park made its presence felt in the year 1981.
    The terrain of the national park is extremely rugged and consists of fascinating deep valleys, sandstone peaks.

  • Prime Attractions--Black Buck, Leopard, Wild Dogs, Indian Bison, Malabar Giant Squirrel, Crocodiles.There are a variety of birds.
     Hornbills and peafowl are common birds found here.The flora consists of mainly sal, teak, tendu.
